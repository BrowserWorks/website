---
title: Grant camera access to Waterfox for Android
label: Grant camera access to Waterfox for Android
---

Waterfox's built-in QR code reader lets you [visit web pages scanned from a QR code when available](/scan-qr-codes-firefox-android). Waterfox will need access to your camera in order to scan codes. Here's how to change it in your settings:

1.  Open your Android settings by tapping the Settings app on your device.
2.  Tap Apps & notifications (this might have a different label on your version of Android).
3.  Tap **Waterfox**.
4.  Tap Permissions.
5.  Tap Camera.
6.  Tap Allow.
7.  Close out of the Settings app, reopen Waterfox and scan the QR code.
